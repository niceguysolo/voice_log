How Voice Log Works

Your daily memory assistant, simplified

📱 What is Voice Log?

Voice Log is a simple, voice-first app designed specifically for older adults to easily track daily activities. Whether it's medications, meals, exercise, or doctor's appointments, just speak and we'll remember for you.

No complicated interfaces. No typing required. Just press record, speak naturally, and we handle the rest.

✨ Key Features

🎤

Voice Recording

Just speak naturally - no typing needed. "I took my blood pressure pill" or "Had lunch at noon"

⌨️

Text Option

Prefer typing? That works too! Switch between voice and text anytime.

🤖

AI Assistant

Ask questions about your logs: "What did I eat yesterday?" or "When did I last take my medication?"

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Alerts

Add a family member who'll get notified when you log activities - perfect for peace of mind.

📊

Activity History

See your logged activities organized by date. Search and review past entries easily.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your data is encrypted and secure. Only you and your family member can access it.

🚀 How to Use Voice Log

1

Sign In

Create your account with just your email and name. Start with a free 7-day trial - no credit card required.

2

Record Your Activity

Click the microphone button and speak: "I took my morning pills" or "Walked for 20 minutes." We'll transcribe it automatically.

3

Ask Questions

Use the AI assistant to ask about your activities: "Did I take my medication today?" or "What did I have for breakfast?"

4

Add Family Member (Optional)

Invite a family member who can view your logs and get peace of mind that you're staying active and healthy.

5

Review History

See all your logged activities organized by date. Search for specific entries or review patterns over time.

👥 For Family Members

Family members can create their own login to view their loved one's activities. This is perfect for:

  • Checking if mom took her medications
  • Seeing if dad went for his daily walk
  • Monitoring meal patterns and nutrition
  • Peace of mind for caregivers

Family members log in with their own email and a 4-digit passcode, then can view all logged activities in real-time.
